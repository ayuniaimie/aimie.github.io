Experience in education

Both of my parent encourage all of us to futher our study and achieve our dream.They are really helpful to whenever I need any help. I am grateful to have such a loving and caring family. I was just 13 years old and of course my family did not allow me to go that far so after I rejected the offer, I applied for SMKA Dato Haji Abbas and luckily I got it. I studied there for 5 years starting from 2014 until 2018.
